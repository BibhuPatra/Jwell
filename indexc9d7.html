The owner of "A Spa for you" is who provided my services. If you are truly looking for a customized massage and facial I would highly recommend. My services were outstanding. She interviewed me for 30 min before we started to understand my areas of tensions and preferences. Very cool!
With that in mind though, if you are looking for a very "spa feel", ie. the locker room, showers, fancy waiting/relaxing area, water with cucumber, hot tub, etc. this is not your spot. The spa is located in an apartment complex that is business and residential--therefore the location is not glamorous. The room & bathroom are simple and clean. The space is extremely small. You do hear outside noise.
I was happy to support a local, small business owner.
